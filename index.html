    About  Cowie

    Cowie is a versatile anime bot for Discord built with the intention of catching the attention of anime enthusiasts. It can post random anime images, clips, quotes and can even play music. Yes! You heard it right! Cowie can play music despite being an anime bot. It does not have moderation features yet. But I am working on it and will be implemented. Given its extensive features, Cowie is a bot that you shouldn't miss out on for your Discord servers.

    I am mohade_jr#6593, and I developed Cowie. It was October 2022 when I finally completed its development and released the bot for public use. Cowie is one of my hobby projects, and I cherish it. I will update the bot based on user feedback and requests. So, make sure that you join Cowie's community server and become a part of the cause that will one day make Cowie one of the best Discord bots on this planet.
